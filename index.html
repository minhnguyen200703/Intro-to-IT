ABOUT US

Rate My Professors boasts more than 19 million ratings of over 1.7 million professors from college students like you. Its database includes more than 7,500 schools across the United States, Canada, England, Scotland, and Wales. Simply type the name of a professor in the search bar or find your school by entering its name. You can read and leave reviews as a member or guest. Either way, you'll remain anonymous when rating professors on the platform. Displayed on every school's page are the ratings of its top professors, as well as the average professor ratings and how that school stacks up to similar institutions.
