ABOUT US

3R Education would be an assistive space for RMIT students which include a wealth of useful information to help them cope with the stress that comes with being disoriented. This website, which is geared especially for first-year students, provides instructors’ ratings, roadmap and study resources so that students could choose their ideal professors and make the best strategy for their university life. Its database has all of the information on professors, materials for every course so all you have to do is simply type your lecturers’ or courses’ name and it will provide you with all of the relevant information. Furthermore, if you are unsure which course you should study in that semester, you may look for a suggested study map offered by seniors here.

College is always like a new chapter in one's life, and everything will be different from what you had experienced at high school. Let's make your university life easier now. We take a huge pride to serve you.
